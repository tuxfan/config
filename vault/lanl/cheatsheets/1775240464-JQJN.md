## Notes

- Some mappings are context-specific: Oil buffers, LSP-attached buffers, quickfix buffers, fugitive merge buffers, multicursor layer, and the local diff operator.
- `<leader>up` is defined twice in the current config. The later Snacks mapping for No Neck Pain overrides the earlier autopairs toggle, so `<leader>up` currently toggles No Neck Pain.
- `<leader>n` is defined twice but both mappings open notification history, so behavior is unchanged.
